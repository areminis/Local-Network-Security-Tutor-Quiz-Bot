Lecture 17 – MAC vs Digital Signatures (DETAILED + ELABORATED)

MAC:
• Uses shared key
• Provides integrity + authentication
• No non-repudiation

Digital Signatures:
• Uses asymmetric keys
• Provides authenticity + integrity + non-repudiation
• Signer cannot deny participation


Comparison:
MAC:
- Fast
- Key management harder (shared secrets)

Signatures:
- Slower
- Easily verifiable using public key
