Lecture 18 – Digital Signature Standards & RSA-PSS (DETAILED + ELABORATED)

Overview:
Digital signature standards define how to securely sign data.


RSA Signatures:
S = (H(M))^d mod n
Verify: H(M) == S^e mod n


PKCS #1 v2.2:
• Formal standard for RSA signatures + encryption
• Defines padding (RSA-PSS, OAEP)

RSA-PSS:
• Probabilistic signature
• More secure than deterministic RSA
• Uses salt + randomness → prevents attacks
